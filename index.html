Residential Cleaning Service

At Osorio Cleaning & Maintenance LLC, we offer professional and reliable residential cleaning services to ensure your home stays clean, organized, and healthy. We offer deep cleaning and light cleaning options, adapting to your needs and the level of maintenance your space requires.

Residential Cleaning 🔹 Light Cleaning

Ideal for regular home maintenance, helping to keep spaces clean and organized without dirt accumulation.

  • Sweeping, mopping, and vacuuming floors.
  • Dusting furniture and surfaces.
  • Cleaning bathrooms (sink, toilet, shower, and mirrors)
  • Kitchen cleaning (exterior of appliances, sink, surfaces)
  • Changing bed linens and organizing beds.
  • Taking out the trash and replacing bags.
  • Cleaning interior windows (accessible)

Residential Cleaning 🔹 Deep Cleaning

Recommended for homes that need a more detailed cleaning, especially after long periods without maintenance or during key moments like moving or special events.

  • Everything included in light cleaning, plus.
  • Deep cleaning of bathrooms and kitchens (including tiles, behind appliances and furniture)
  • Dusting and cleaning ceiling fans, light fixtures, and baseboards.
  • Deep cleaning of accessible interior and exterior windows.
  • Removal of grease buildup in the kitchen.
  • Cleaning of doors, light switches, and handles.
  • Vacuuming and deep cleaning of carpets and upholstery (as needed)
  • Closet cleaning and basic space organization (optional)

Handyman Services

At Osorio Cleaning & Maintenance LLC, we offer reliable and professional handyman services to assist you with minor repairs and maintenance in your home, office, or commercial property. We specialize in jobs that do not require a license, ensuring practical, efficient solutions at affordable prices.

What services do we provide?

  • Minor repairs on doors and windows (adjustments, lock installation, hinge replacement)
  • Interior and exterior painting (touch-ups and full application)
  • Drywall repair and patching (small cracks and holes)
  • Replacement and installation of handles, knobs, and locks.
  • Cleaning and maintenance of outdoor areas (patios, terraces, sidewalks)
  • Pressure washing (Pressure Washing) on driveways, facades, and sidewalks.
  • Sealing cracks in floors and walls.
  • Furniture maintenance and assembly of new products.
  • Installation and replacement of ceiling fans (as long as it’s just the mounting, no electrical changes)
  • Replacing light bulbs and basic lighting fixtures.
  • Replacement of toilet seats and bathroom accessories.
  • Replacing faucets and showerheads (without modifying pipes or internal connections)
  • Basic organization and storage improvements (closets, pantries, garages)
